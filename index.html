Frequently Asked Questions

How can your legal firm assist with RERA complaints in Uttar Pradesh?

Our firm specializes in RERA complaints, guiding you through the entire process—from filing the complaint to representing you in hearings. We ensure your rights as a homebuyer are protected.

What types of issues can we file a RERA complaint for?

You can file complaints regarding project delays, misrepresentation of property details, non-compliance with RERA regulations, and disputes with developers in Uttar Pradesh.

What documents are needed to file a RERA complaint?

You will need your booking receipt, project details, communication with the builder, and any relevant documents supporting your claim. Our team can help you gather and organize these documents.

What is the fees for filing a RERA complaint through your firm?

We charge Rs.1500/- for examination of documents and legal opinion, Rs. 3500/- for legal notice & Rs. 25000/- + Expenses for the complaint case.

Can you represent me in RERA hearings?

Yes, we provide representation as an advocate in RERA hearings to ensure your case is presented effectively and your rights are secured. Our experienced lawyers are here to advocate for you.

What should I do if my complaint is rejected?

If your complaint is rejected, we can help you file an appeal and re-evaluate your case for any further action. We’re committed to ensuring your concerns are addressed.

How can I contact your firm for assistance?

You can reach us through direct call, whatsapp or visit our office. Our team is ready to assist you with any queries regarding RERA complaints.

Why should I choose your firm for RERA legal assistance?

Our firm has extensive experience in RERA cases, a dedicated team of experts, and a proven track record of successful resolutions. We prioritize client satisfaction and deliver results.
